About Us

Designer stones is a sourcing and fabricating company that specializes in solid stone surface countertops. We offer top-of-the-line custom-made reliable stone products designed, fabricated, and installed by our talented experts using state-of-the-art equipment. We can mold and craft any solid stones into your desired design. We are also equipped necessary logistical infrastructure to deliver and install your countertops in a cost-effective, fast, and reliable manner. We make it a point to tailor our products and services according to your individual needs and requirements, whether it involves projects for residential and commercial establishments, hospitals, schools, banks, airports, restaurants, hotels, and many more.

Our products and services are ideal for customers who require high-quality and distinctive custom designs for kitchen countertops, bathroom vanities, sinks and bowls, fireplaces as well as reception counters, wall and column cladding, countertops and tables, and partitions, and elevator floors. To supplement our top-quality products, we seek to be a distinguished provider of the most flexible and reliable customer service to cater to every client's need. Beauty, durability, and professional installation accompany every countertop purchased at Designer Stones.
