About Us

Designer stones is a sourcing and fabricating company that specialists in solid stone surface counter tops. We offer top-of-the-line custom-made solid stone products designed, fabricated and installed by our talented experts by using state-of-the-art equipments. We can mould and craft any solid stones into your desired design. We are also equipped necessary logistical infrastructure to deliver and install your counter tops in a cost -effective, fast, and reliable manner. We make it a point to tailor our products and services according to your individual needs and requirements, whether it involves projects for residential and commercial establishments, hospitals, schools, banks, airports, restaurants, hotels, and many more.

Our products and services are ideal for customers who require high-quality and distinctive custom designs for kitchen counter tops, bathroom vanities, sinks and bowls, fireplace as well as reception counters, wall and column cladding, counter tops and tables, and partitions and elevator floors. To supplement our top-quality products, we seek to be a distinguished provider of the most flexible and reliable customer service to cater to every client need. Beauty, durability, and professional installation - these accompany every counter top purchased at Designer Stones.
